My Favourite Recipe: Chocolate Biscuit Cake

Ingredients:

  • 10oz Butter
  • 150ml Golden Syrup
  • 8oz Cooking chocolate
  • 200g Digestive Biscuits
  • 200g Rich Tea Biscuits
  • 4 Crunchie bars
  • 1 packet of Maltessors

Method:

  1. Melt the Butter, syrup and chocolate over a low heat
  2. Crush teh biscuits
  3. Cruch the crunchies
  4. Add the biscuits to the melted mixture and mix well
  5. Add the crushed Crunchies and packet of maltessors
  6. Place in a greased tin and refrigerate.
